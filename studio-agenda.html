Studio Week 1

In class: Introduction, what is design, what is communication design, what is interaction design; all-class exercise on goals, strengths; small-group ideation exercise (how might we design a product that helps faculty remember names)

Reading: Introduction to Are We Human? by Beatriz Colomina and Mark Wigley

Assignment: Begin Project 1: Typographic Dialogues. Find a clip of movie dialogue that is meaningful to you. The dialogue should be a conversation between at least two characters, have a beginning, a middle, and an end, and last more than one minute. The movie can be in any language (as long as you’re willing to translate) (pro tip: Rather than googling “best movie dialogue,” I would suggest thinking if there is a scene you remember often from a movie, and that you’d like to return to, know more about, and visualize.)
